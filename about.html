Our Legacy

Redefining the art of fine dining through innovation, passion, and obsidian elegance.

The Beginning

Tannu's Kitchen began with a singular vision: to create a dining space where culinary art meets architectural beauty. Founded in 2001 by Executive Chef Tannu, the restaurant quickly became a sanctuary for food connoisseurs.

We believe that dining is not just about sustenance, but about storytelling. Every dish that leaves our pass tells a story of heritage, sourcing, and meticulous preparation.

"We don't just cook food; we engineer experiences. The plate is our canvas, and the ingredients are our paint."

- CHEF TANNU, FOUNDER

The Philosophy

Our philosophy is rooted in "Obsidian Precision." We combine dark, elegant aesthetics with vibrant, explosive flavors. We source our ingredients from local organic farms and sustainable fisheries, ensuring that our impact on the planet is as gentle as our service.

From the moment you step through our doors, you are not just a customer; you are a guest in our home.
